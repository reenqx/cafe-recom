Bakerang

By Nurin KK • on Aug 30, 2024

Review by me

The café is beautifully decorated with plenty of photo-worthy spots to capture memories. The ambiance is warm and inviting, making it the perfect place to relax and enjoy a leisurely meal. The breakfast and brunch sets are easy to order, offering a variety of delicious options to suit every taste. Everything served, from the food and desserts to the beverages, is carefully made in-house, ensuring top quality and freshness.

One of the standout items on the menu is the cheese tart, which has become the café's signature dish. The flavor is perfectly balanced, with a rich creaminess and just the right amount of sweetness, leaving a lasting impression on anyone who tries it.

In addition to the fantastic food, the staff are incredibly nice and friendly, always ready to provide excellent service. Their warm hospitality adds to the overall charm of the café, making it an experience you'll want to repeat again and again.
