Three dollars

By Nurin KK • on Aug 30, 2024

Review by me

Three Dollars is not just an adorable café but also a flower shop. The café is beautifully decorated with dried flowers in various corners, offering visitors plenty of photo opportunities.

What's even more special is that the décor changes with each season, ensuring that every visit to Three Dollars leaves a lasting impression.

As for the menu, the café focuses on serving easy-to-enjoy pastries, along with a selection of teas and coffees. Only high-quality ingredients are used, with each dish and drink often adorned with flowers, making this a signature touch of the café. This thoughtful decoration allows everyone to fully immerse themselves in the café’s delightful atmosphere.
