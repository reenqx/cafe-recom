Sunday morning

By Nurin KK • on Aug 30, 2024

Review by me

Sunday Morning Café is a charming little café located in Mae Sot district. The exterior is adorned with a variety of lush green plants that beautifully contrast with the deep black door. Inside, the café offers a minimalist ambiance with a clean palette of white and black.

The highlight of the café is its delectable bakery menu, featuring visually appealing and delicious pastries baked fresh daily. Recommended items include Garlic Cream Cheese Bread, Croffle Mango Sticky Rice, Cheese Cups, Tiramisu Cake, Fukuoka Cheese Cake, and Mae Sot Milk Tea. Each treat promises to be both aesthetically pleasing and mouthwateringly good.
